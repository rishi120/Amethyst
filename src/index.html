About Amethyst

At Amethyst, healing isn’t just about getting better — it’s about feeling stronger, more confident, and independent again. We provide personalized, evidence-based care to help our clients restore mobility, reduce pain, and live healthier, more fulfilling lives.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves focused and compassionate care.

Our Vision

To inspire a healthier, stronger, and pain-free life for everyone.

Our Goal

Healing and empowering through movement and care.

Meet Our Team

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Dr. Pooja Bhagabati and Dr. Nikita Gangurde, Amethyst was born out of a shared passion for physiotherapy and a commitment to improving lives through movement and care. With years of experience in the field, our founders envisioned a clinic that not only provided top-notch physiotherapy services but also fostered a supportive and empowering environment for clients.

Our Expertise

Our expertise lies in providing comprehensive care tailored to each individual's unique needs.

Physical Therapy

Our physical therapy services focus on restoring mobility and function through targeted exercises and manual therapy.

Occupational Therapy

We help individuals regain independence in daily activities through specialized interventions.

Rehabilitation

Our rehabilitation programs are designed to support recovery from injuries and surgeries.
