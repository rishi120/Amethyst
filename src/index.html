Our Story

Amethyst Physiotherapy was founded with a single goal—delivering high-quality care in the comfort of your home. Established in 2025 by Dr. Pooja Bhagabati and Dr. Nikita Gangurde, Amethyst grew from a shared love for physiotherapy and a desire to help people move better and live healthier lives. Drawing on years of experience, our founders set out to build a clinic that offers high-quality care in a welcoming, supportive, and empowering space.
