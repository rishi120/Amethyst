About Amethyst Geriatric Solutions

Amethyst Geriatric Solutions is dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services tailored to the unique needs of older adults. Our multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals specializes in geriatric care, offering personalized treatment plans that promote healthy aging and improve the quality of life for our elderly patients. We focus on managing chronic conditions, preventing falls, and enhancing overall well-being through compassionate and evidence-based care.

Our care focus

Specialized support tailored to aging needs

Explore the core areas we prioritize to help older adults stay safe, active, and independent.

Mobility, balance, and fall-risk reduction

We assess gait, lower-limb strength, and home-risk factors to reduce fall incidents and improve confidence in everyday movement.

  • Balance and gait retraining
  • Assistive device and walking strategy support
  • Home-safety recommendations for safer mobility

Cognition-first care and routine support

For older adults with memory concerns, we structure care plans around clarity, consistency, and family-inclusive decision support.

  • Early cognitive screening and tracking
  • Daily routine planning for safety and orientation
  • Family education for long-term continuity

Integrated management of chronic health issues

We coordinate ongoing care for long-term conditions while preserving function, reducing flare-ups, and improving quality of life.

  • Medication and symptom review with care plans
  • Strength, endurance, and functional conditioning
  • Regular follow-ups with measurable progress goals

Practical caregiver guidance and support

We help families navigate daily care needs with practical strategies that reduce stress and improve patient safety.

  • Safe transfer and mobility handling techniques
  • Emergency preparedness and escalation planning
  • Caregiver burnout prevention and support pathways
