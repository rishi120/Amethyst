About Amethyst Orthopedic Solutions

Amethyst orthopedic solutions are designed to provide optimal support and comfort for individuals with post surgical issues. Whether you are recovering from surgery or seeking relief from chronic pain, Amethyst has a range of orthopedic solutions to meet your needs.

Who this service is for

Designed for people who need structured orthopedic recovery

This service is ideal if pain, stiffness, or injury is limiting your daily life, sports performance, or post-surgery progress.

Post-Surgical Recovery

For people recovering after joint replacement, ligament repair, fracture fixation, or spine-related procedures.

Recovery milestones are sequenced to protect healing tissue, reduce swelling, and restore confidence in daily movement.

Joint & Muscle Pain

For persistent knee, shoulder, hip, back, or neck pain that affects mobility, work, or sleep quality.

Programs target painful movement patterns and strength deficits so work, sleep, and routine tasks become easier.

Sports & Activity Injuries

For sprains, strains, tendon issues, and overuse injuries in active adults and athletes.

Sport-specific progressions rebuild control, power, and joint stability before full return to training.

Limited Movement

For stiffness, weakness, and reduced range of motion that make walking, climbing stairs, or lifting difficult.

Mobility-first treatment improves joint range and reduces compensations that make stairs and lifting harder.

Age-Related Orthopedic Care

For seniors needing safe strength, balance, and joint-focused rehabilitation to maintain independence.

Balance, coordination, and functional strength are trained safely to support independent everyday living.

Recurring Orthopedic Problems

For people with repeated flare-ups who need a long-term, guided plan to prevent re-injury.

Long-term maintenance plans focus on flare-up control, movement quality, and reinjury prevention.
